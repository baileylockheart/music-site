About Me:

I'm a singer/songwriter from Las Vegas! I play guitar, ukulele, bass, and piano, but I'm always learning. (Self-Taught)

I wrote my first song when I was nine years old, and I'm still doing it today! If you asked me what motivated that very first song, the answer is "because I could" but I like to think that I have a reason now.

It bothers me when I get a specific feeling from a topic or idea, and I can't find enough songs that fit that feeling. I want to write songs to fill those holes, and it makes me happy when I manage to do so!

Equipment Used:

Microphone: Rockville RCM SR
Interface: Behringer U-Phoria UM2
DAW: Garageband, BandLab, Soundtrap
Acoustic Treatment: MouKey MPFBK3

I do all my recording in my bedroom :)
